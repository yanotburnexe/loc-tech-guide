Module 05

ICU MessageFormat & CLDR new

ICU MessageFormat is the industry-standard syntax used by React Intl, Angular, Crowdin, Phrase, and others for plurals, gender selection, number/date formatting, and nested messages. CLDR is the underlying Unicode data repository that defines plural categories, date patterns, and locale conventions for 200+ locales. This module deserves serious time — it sits at the intersection of engineering and translation.
